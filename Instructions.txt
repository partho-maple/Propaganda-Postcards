---------------------	Setting Up ti.storekit	---------------------

1. First of all make sure that your apps GUID is unique. You will find GUID into your apps tiapp.xml file.

2. Copy your apps GUID and replace the GUID of manifest fine whch you will find into ../Library/Application Support/Titanium/modules/iphone/ti.storekit/3.0.0/manifest

Thats it.
